Anime character types ▼

ツンデレ Tsundere: character who treats the one they love poorly

クーデレ kuudere: character who is cold, emotionless and cynical

だんデレ Dandere: character who is silent and keeps to themselves

ヤンデレ Yandere: character who uses violence to control their love interest

うつデレ Utsudere: character who is sad and depressed

ウンデレ Undere: character who says yes to everything their crush says

さどデレ Sadodere: character who enjoys manipulating people’s feelings

まやデレ Mayadere: character who switches sides after falling in love

かねデレ Kanedere: character who is attracted to others with money or status

かみデレ Kamidere: character with a god complex - proud and arrogant

ボデレ Bodere: character who fights because they can't articulate their desires

ばかデレ Bakadere: character who is very clumsy and stupid

ひきく

The english translation of ひきく is Hikiku. Hikiku is a combination of the two words ひきこもり [hikikomori] and オタク [Otaku]. Hikikomori refers to reclusive adolescents or adults who withdraw from society. Otaku refers to a person who is obsessed with anime or manga.
